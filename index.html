Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Welcome to SabbathGPT, a Christian chatbot designed to provide faith-based insights and information. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your information.

1. Information Collection

SabbathGPT does not require users to sign up or provide personal credentials to access its features. Most data is stored locally on the user's device. However, certain information is collected to enhance user experience and improve AI response quality.

a. User-Provided Information

Users may voluntarily provide the following:

  • Text messages sent to the AI
  • Reports on AI-generated content (e.g., false or misleading responses)

b. Automatically Collected Information

For performance and analytics purposes, we may collect:

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Basic app usage statistics
  • Anonymized interaction data

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is solely used to improve the SabbathGPT experience. This includes:

  • Enhancing AI response accuracy
  • Improving app functionality
  • Developing new features
  • Moderating and refining content quality
  • Ensuring compliance with legal regulations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. However, anonymized and aggregated data may be shared with:

  • Service providers assisting in app maintenance
  • Analytics platforms for performance insights
  • Research institutions studying AI interactions

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement security measures to protect user data:

  • Messages are temporarily stored for AI improvements
  • Data is encrypted during transmission
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Collected data is periodically deleted

5. Third-Party Services

a. Google Mobile Ads

SabbathGPT uses Google AdMob for in-app advertisements. Ads may be targeted based on anonymized data, but no personally identifiable information is shared.

b. Firebase Remote Configuration

Firebase is used for dynamic app configurations and updates. This service does not collect personal user data.

6. User Controls

Users have control over their interactions with the app, including:

  • Option to delete conversation history
  • Ability to report and remove specific messages
  • Control over saving favorite messages

7. Compliance

SabbathGPT complies with applicable privacy laws, including:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA): The app is designed for general audiences, and parental consent is required for users under 18.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained based on the following policies:

  • Conversation history is stored for up to 30 days.
  • Users can manually delete conversations at any time.
  • Anonymized interaction data may be retained for research and app improvement.

9. Updates and Notifications

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements or legal changes. Users will be notified of significant updates, and continued app usage implies acceptance of the revised policy.
